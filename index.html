Roulette game

This game of roulette is a game to test how lucky you are.
There are some rules that you need to be aware of before playing this game.

  • You will be given a hundred dollars to start the game.
  • You can choose either 1 or 5 dollars to bet on the board.
  • After more than 30 dollars have been played on the table, a winning number will appear.
  • If you made a bet on a winning number, you will win money in the multiples of 34.
  • The odd and even will only win you twice as much as you bet.
  • You can press play more button to continue.
  • If your budget is 0 or cannot make a bet of 30 dollars on the table, you can press play again for a fresh start.
  • If you are ready to play, press Play here button to start
